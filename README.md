## 📚 RESEARCH SCOPE

**Objective:** Understand the fundamental chemical mechanisms by which plasma breaks molecular bonds and enhances combustion efficiency.

**Original Paper:** Focused on bond-breaking chemistry at molecular level
**Key Finding:** Plasma provides targeted energy to weaken/break specific chemical bonds before combustion

---

## 🔬 FUNDAMENTAL MECHANISMS STUDIED

### 1. Molecular Bond Dissociation
- **C-H bonds** in hydrocarbons (CH4, C2H4, RP-1)
- **H-H bonds** in molecular hydrogen
- **O=O bonds** in molecular oxygen
- **Activation energies** reduced by plasma pre-treatment

### 2. Radical Formation
- **H• radicals** from H2 dissociation
- **OH• radicals** from H2O plasma treatment
- **CH3•, CH2•, CH• radicals** from hydrocarbon cracking
- **O• atoms** from O2 dissociation

### 3. Ignition Enhancement
- **Reduced ignition delay** by 35-45%
- **Lower ignition temperature** requirements
- **Improved flame stability** at lean/rich mixtures
- **Eliminated flashback/blowout** in certain conditions
